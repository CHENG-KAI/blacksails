ABOUT

Black Sails Technology is committed to developing young talent in the film industry. We are developing a creative media and resource sharing platform that features original content including online shows, series and short films with the goal of providing a supportive system for aspiring filmmakers. We are dedicated to providing an integrated platform for filmmakers and movie producers that includes video creation, video distribution and video marketing.

Black Sails Technology was founded in the San Francisco Bay Area city of Pleasanton. Currently located in the Silicon Valley, a place immersed in entrepreneurship and resources for science and technology, Black Sails is fortunate to start and grow our business here.
